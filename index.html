<h2>About Me</h2>

<p>class=“green-paragraph”>I first took an interest in photography in 2007. I did not know much about it other than I loved to take photographs. At the time, I used the standard pocket digital cameras most people had.</p>


<p> I didn’t start to take my photography seriously until 2015. That year, I started operating under the name Jess Leh Photography. I spent more time on my craft, learning new techniques and how to use a more advanced camera. I asked a friend to model for me so I could gain more practice and start a portfolio and for my first shoot, I was pleasantly surprised with the results. See for yourself!</p>

<p> Once I was able to show off some of my work from the first shoot, I started receiving requests from other people. It was mostly family shoots, but there were some requests for street/fashion shoots as well. I think of the various experiences I’ve had, I like working with kids the most. They are so unpredictable and it makes for some pretty fun pictures.</p>
